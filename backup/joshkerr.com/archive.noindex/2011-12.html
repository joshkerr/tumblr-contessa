Movie theater loosing revenue?

Great article by Roger Ebert on why movie theatres are loosing revenues. He summarizes how the high cost of going to the theatre is in competition with the increasing options for watching at home. In the end he says that theatres that add extra value will continue to thrive and those that depend on big blockbusters to drive people in will fail.

Case in point, the iPic theatre chain or the Alamo Drafthouse.

How awesome is Reddit?

I’ve been a Reddit user for a few years now. I love the site. It is the best example of community contributed content on the Internet and sometimes really magical things happen on it. Today was one such experience.

It started with an AMA thread which means “Ask Me Anything.” Typically someone will post something like “I’m a famous author, AMA.” People then ask all sorts of questions in the comments to which the author will answer. Today there was a request for “Salman Khan founder of the Khan Academy.” There are many requests for famous people and many do take on the challenge and will post an AMA.

Today Salman Khan happened to learn about the AMA and agreed to the request. He started his thread with a photo of himself with this Reddit handle in order to prove that he was in fact the real Salman Khan.
