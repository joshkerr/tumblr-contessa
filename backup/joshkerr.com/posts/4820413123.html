nvALT launches 2.0 with HTML preview support

nvALT is a fork of Notational Velocity a powerful note taking system. What I like about nvALT over Notational Velocity is that it seems to be geared more for bloggers. The 2.0 version takes that even further by giving you a customizable HTML/CSS preview window. You can add your blog’s theme and now get an accurate preview when calling up your preview window.

I write my blog entries in Markdown because they are easier to write and read than HTML. Sure I can use a visual editor, but I like the power and control of writing source, a by product of my years as a coder.

Notational Velocity has other powerful features as well.
