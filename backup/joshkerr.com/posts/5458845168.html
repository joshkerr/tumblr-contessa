Change your icons in Google Chrome extensions.

I did not know this, but you can change the icons on your Chrome browser extension bar. Some of these extensions have really bad icons and I hide them right away. Now that I know I can customize them, maybe they can come back.

On a mac: 

~/Library/Application Support/Google/Chrome/Default/Extensions/

Just look for the extension in that list of folder gibberish. Inside you’ll see a icon_128.png. Replace that, restart Chrome and you are good to go.
