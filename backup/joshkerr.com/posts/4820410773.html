NYC subway at 42nd St, a photo by joshkerr on Flickr.

I took this photo with Instagram. I’m really digging this app.

I’m also digging the new photo sharing feature in Flickr.  It places a one click button at the top of your Flickr photos making sharing easy as pie.

The icons are a bit lame, especially for a photo sharing service as popular as Flickr, but hey, its a Yahoo company.

I guess I should be happy that Flickr is still around.  It was only two months ago that I downloaded all of my photos from the service thinking that Yahoo was about to fold.  Now I’m using <a href=”http://backupify.com”>Backupify</a> to back things up so I guess I’m safe.  Wow, talk about a rant of a post.
