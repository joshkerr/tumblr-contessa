How to pitch your startup to the press

Pitching your company to the technology media is no easy task.  I’ve had to do this on a number of occasions with mixed results.  When successful, there is huge upside, but there is also a significant risk.  Creating a compelling story is key to getting a writer interested, but the hard part in my opinion is getting your company’s strategic goals into that story.
