Mercedes-Benz iPad integration makes no sense.

Why would Mercedes Benz strap an iPad on the back of a headrest and call it an integration?  Why can’t I buy my own iPad and strap it there myself?  It doesn’t seem to difficult.  Plus, why would I want to surf the web with my iPad attached to the seat?  Its more comfortable to have it in my lap.

If there was a way to AirPlay my iPad to a TV monitor on the back of the seat, that might be a cool integration, but the way things stand now, it is a waste of money.
