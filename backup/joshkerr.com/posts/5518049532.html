I'm 37 and I still like Legos.

I’ve got two young kids, a 6 year old girl and a 3 year old boy. I was recently at the Lego store in my local mall with my kids buying them Legos, the little building blocks that I loved as a kid. They haven’t changed much over the years, but I found myself falling in love with them all over again.

My daughter loves Star Wars something she certainly got from me and they had the Imperial Shuttle Craft and it was really cool.

I didn’t buy it, but I plan to go back and get it. I mean, how awesome does that thing look? My kids are all over Legos now so I’ve got a great excuse to play with them too.
