Wrapped up taping for a TV show pilot

I’ve never been featured in a TV show before so when I received a call from a producer wanting to create a series based on entrepreneurs with me as the first, I was shocked. Once the initial shock subsided, I felt fear.

Its not that I’m afraid of being in the spotlight, far from it actually, I’ve been on the stage in front of hundreds of people either making a speech, or demoing a product. What scared me is that this great opportunity was placed in my lap and I’m certainly not the most successful entrepreneur in Austin. There are countless CEO’s who’ve achieved more, done more and should receive this honor in place of me.

I’m a big fan of the Austin startup community and a TV show featuring Austin entrepreneurs is a great way to further the growth of this community. I’m honored to be the one chosen to kick off the series.

After a month of planning, the filming happened this past Friday. I started at the Harris Hill Race track to shoot footage of me driving around the track (something I love to do in my off time) and then shifted to my home where they I participated in an in-depth interview. It was a lot of fun, but I suspect even more exciting when I can actually watch it on TV.
