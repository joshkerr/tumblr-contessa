Fast Society: Keep up with your friends at a concert

Fast society is an iPhone app that lets you text message a group of friends but only for a short period of time.  The app is great for concerts, conventions or any other short lived event where you’d want to group message or call people, but once the event is over, don’t want to be bothered.
