Watch MKV videos on your iPad & iPhone with this script.

I wrote a simple shell script to convert MKV video files into M4V (iTunes and iOS) compatible files. The script is easy to use and will process all MKV files in a given folder. It can be easily enabled using folder actions in Mac OSX.
