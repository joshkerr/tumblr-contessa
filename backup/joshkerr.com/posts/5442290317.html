Comcast Offers Help to The Pirate Bay, Problems Fixed | TorrentFreak

I really don’t know what to say about this.  Comcast was getting bad PR yesterday for blocking subscribers connection to Pirate Bay.  It turns out that they did not block the connection that in fact there was a third party ISP that had a configuration problem.  Comcast reached out to engineers at Pirate Bay to solve the problem.  This was not at all the outcome I expected in this story.
