Did Apple pull Mac OS Lion Preview 3?  I went to upgrade my notebook from Preview 2 to 3 and it wasn’t in the software update list.  A quick Google search produced this article which says that Apple pulled it due to boot problems with certain configurations.  With WWDC looming, it doesn’t seem like Apple will be able to to complete the OS before the conference.

Update Actually, they did not pull the update. Apple makes it really hard to tell if you are running preview 1 or 2. I was running 1 and preview 3 only shows up for preview 2 users. A quick upgrade and it worked.
