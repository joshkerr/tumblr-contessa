Scale that sends your weight to the Internet?

So check this out, its a scale that automatically sends your weight to a database on the Internet.

Why would I want this? I currently have a digital scale and it memorizes the last weight in its own internal memory. I’m really good a jumping off the scale before it can determine my exact weight so that it doesn’t commit any of my weights to memory. I just don’t want a log of how much I weight, that is personal information between me and the scale and certainly not the rest of the Internet.

I don’t understand what problem this scale is solving.
