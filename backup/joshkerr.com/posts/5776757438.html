It is expensive, but  the Tokyo Pack from Lexdray is one hell of a bag.  Take a bag chock full of compartments and then add more compartments and when you are done, you’ve got this bag.  Made of high quality ballistic nylon and what feels like wet suit material.  It just barely fits the 17” Macbook Pro, but it is a tight fit and will probably be annoying at the TSA checkpoint.  This could be the best backpack I’ve ever owned.
