Tweetie, twitter and Loren, you guys rock

I purchased the MacHeist bundle over the summer with the promise that I’d get to use the new Tweeite for the Mac beta.  I’m a big Tweetie fan and I’ve been awaiting a new version for a while.  Around this time Twitter bought Tweetie and the developer Loren went to work on the web version of Twitter.  

No Tweetie beta was released and all of the MacHeist users were essentially left out in the cold.  Until today.  Tweetie 2 was released to the Mac AppStore and with it comes a surprise.  If you click on the help menu item while holding down option+control+command you’ll be prompted with a screen that lets you enter your email and MacHeist serial number.  Do this and you get this screen:
