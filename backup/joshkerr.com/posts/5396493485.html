Google Announces Chromebooks For Business, At $28 Monthly Per User

Said Pichai, “Many companies have a hard time upgrading OS and most users use laptops today. They take them in and out of enterprise firewalls and this is challenging for IT admins. CIOs and IT admins are moving computing to the cloud.”

via [TechCrunch](http://feedproxy.google.com/~r/Techcrunch/~3/wLZbFcCJa6w/).

The Google Chromebook is either going to be a massive success or abysmal failure.  There is no in between.  I think there is a market for a secure mobile platform, but the power and versatility of the iPad and its massive market of apps is going to hamper the adoption of the Chromebook.  Google’s Chrome app store is paltry in comparison.   Its all about the app stores these days.
