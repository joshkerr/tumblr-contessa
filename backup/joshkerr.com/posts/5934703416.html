Japan shows it has a lot of class

The US donated a ton of support to help the Japanese after the recent earthquake and tsunami. So what does Japan do after the devestating tornado strikes in Joplin? They send $120,000 in relief supplies to the people of Alabama. It is a classy move and one that shows the stength of the relationship between Japan and the US. Nicely done.
