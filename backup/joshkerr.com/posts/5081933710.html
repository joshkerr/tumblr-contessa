Optimizing Nginx for High Traffic Loads | Martin Fjordvald

If you plan to deploy a high traffic site you are probably going to take a look at Nginx.  Out of the box, this web server might not be configured properly for the type of load you plan to receive.  This tutorial will show you how to optimize it so that load times are short.  This tutorial is for advanced users, most people can get by just fine with the default install.
