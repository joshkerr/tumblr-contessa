Apple files motion ot intervene in Lodsys suit

￼

Lodsys files a bunch of lawsuits against developers who use Apple’s in-app purchase mechanism to unlock premium services. They claim that they own the patent on that technology and even though Apple licenses it for their tools, that each developer using it needs a separate license. Apple decides to file a motion to intervene and protect these developers. Go get em Apple.
