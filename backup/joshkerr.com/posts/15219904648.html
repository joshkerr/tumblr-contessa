CloudFlare writeup

ReadWriteWeb has a great writeup on the recent success of newcomer CloudFlare.

I’ve used CloudFlare for about six months now and the service is really cool. The article focuses on how the shortage of IPV4 addresses and CloudFlares massive network infrastructure allow it to offer IPV6 addresses (which are in huge supply) to customers needing the static IP’s. They created a routable bridge between the two addresses.

This bridge creates a very nice choke point for CloudFlare who would be in a great position to sell access, information, etc.. I’m tracking this company closely.
